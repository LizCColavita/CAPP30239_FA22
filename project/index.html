A Numbers Game

Data from: FiveThirtyEight

Campaign donations of professional sports team owners generally lean toward the right of the political spectrum, and by and large, have decreased significantly between the 2016 and 2020 elections. In aggregation, these trends hold across leagues and across gender of the owner.

However, these averages and aggregations hide some anomalies.The anomalies: the MLB, WNBA, and NFL.
